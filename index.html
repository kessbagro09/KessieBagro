About

I genuinely enjoy finance because it challenges me to analyze data, uncover insights, and transform numbers into meaningful dashboards and reports that drive smarter decisions. With 9+ years of experience in financial management and analysis, I’ve built a strong foundation in pricing, budgeting, revenue, and margin analysis, while leveraging AI-driven automation and process improvements to save time and deliver measurable impact.

My career began in accounting, which sharpened my attention to detail, and over time, working closely with Partners, Executive Directors, and Senior Managers has strengthened my ability to think strategically and communicate effectively with decision-makers. Within my teams, I often lead the charge in identifying opportunities for improvement, championing automation, and driving initiatives that streamline processes.

Beyond finance, I am curious and open to learning how AI and process automation can be applied in other fields such as social media management or marketing. I believe finance provides an excellent foundation for innovation, and I’m excited to keep growing my skills — not only as a finance professional, but as someone committed to building smarter, more efficient ways of working across industries.

On the other hand, I am passionate about volunteering and corporate social responsibility (CSR). I believe that giving back and building strong community relationships is as important as professional success. In 2022, I was recognized as a Social Impact Champion for volunteering and CSR initiatives, and I look forward to contributing to meaningful causes alongside my career growth.
