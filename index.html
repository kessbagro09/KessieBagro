About Me

I genuinely enjoy finance because it challenges me to analyze data, uncover insights, and transform numbers into valuable dashboards and reports that guide smarter decisions. With 9+ years of experience in financial management and analysis, I’ve built a strong foundation in pricing, budgeting, revenue, and margin analysis, while utilizing AI-driven automation and process improvements that save time and deliver measurable impact.

My career began in accounting, which sharpened my attention to detail, and over time, working closely with Partners, Executive Directors, and Senior Managers has developed my ability to think strategically and communicate effectively with decision-makers. I’m often the one in my team who identifies opportunities for improvement, champions automation, and drives initiatives that streamline processes.

Beyond finance, I am curious and open to learning how AI and process automation can be applied in other fields such as social media management or marketing. I also believe in giving back — I served as a Social Impact Champion in 2022, actively joining corporate social responsibility initiatives and volunteering activities.
